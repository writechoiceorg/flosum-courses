# Lesson 4: The Economics of Drift

The seven mismatches translate into concrete organizational costs (headcount, delivery capacity, compliance exposure, and engineer attrition) that compound over time.

* [**Where the Money Leaks**](money-leaking.md): five cost categories with market-sourced numbers: failed deployments, rollback hours, release delays, audit risk, and developer burnout.
* [**The Human Translator Tax**](human-translator-tax.md): why every Git-based Salesforce program funds a full-time reconciliation role, and why that cost scales with complexity, not effort.
* [**The Trust Tax**](trust-tax.md): how a pipeline's failure history generates behavioral slowdowns (slower cadence, more gates, out-of-band routing) that become permanent.
* [**The Break-Even Calculation**](break-even-calculation.md): the formula and worked example for calculating when the alternative pays for itself against accumulated current-state cost.
