# Lesson 6: What Actually Works

Six specific capabilities separate solutions that address root causes from those that manage symptoms: this lesson names them and defines the bar.

* [**Metadata-Native Architecture**](metadata-native-architecture.md): six requirements every adequate Salesforce DevOps tool must satisfy: component-aware VCS, semantic merge, org-aware deployment, first-class rollback, compliance audit trail, and platform-native security.
* [**Extend Git or Replace It?**](extend-git-or-replace.md): two schools agree Git alone fails but disagree on the fix: what each can and can't deliver against the six requirements.
* [**Meet Flosum**](meet-flosum.md): positions Flosum as a metadata-native system designed to meet all six requirements, and points to the specialist and architect courses that cover how.
