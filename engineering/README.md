# Why Salesforce DevOps Needs Unique Treatment

**Why Salesforce DevOps Needs Unique Treatment** is the foundation course in the Flosum certification curriculum. Designed as the entry point for all learners regardless of which subsequent courses they pursue.

Concept-level: teaches why Salesforce DevOps demands a different approach and what kind of solution that demands. No tool operation covered.

***

## Choose your track

* [**Track A — Business Track**](track-a-business-track/overview.md) — For admins, release managers, project leads, RevOps, business analysts, and executives. 4–5 hours. No prerequisites.
* [**Track B — Engineering Track**](track-b-engineering-track/track-b-engineering.md) — For developers, architects, DevOps engineers, and technical leads. 8–10 hours. Requires Git and CI/CD fundamentals.
